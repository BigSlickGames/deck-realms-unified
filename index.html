The Guardians of Compassion

"We fight for love, and love fights for us."

Hearts are the healers and protectors of the Deck Realms. Guided by emotion and unity, they draw strength from the bonds they share with allies. They thrive on defense, recovery, and counterplay — turning pain into power.

Playstyle:

  • Support & Sustain: Heal allies, restore energy, and boost morale.
  • Synergy Focus: Their true power shines in teamwork — the longer they fight together, the stronger they become.
  • Counterattack Masters: They punish those who strike in anger, turning aggression against the attacker.

Ideal For: Players who prefer strategy, protection, and outlasting opponents rather than rushing in headfirst.

♠

The Noble Warriors

"Honor above all, victory through discipline."

Spades are the knights and tacticians of the realm — forged in duty, shaped by discipline. Every move is calculated, every strike measured. They embody strength, order, and unwavering resolve.

Playstyle:

  • Balanced Combat: Strong offense and defense with precision control.
  • Formation Power: Gain bonuses when units or cards are positioned in structured patterns.
  • Momentum Fighters: The longer they hold the line, the deadlier they become.

Ideal For: Players who like tactical combat, control of the battlefield, and predictable, reliable power.

♦

The Seers of Time

"Past. Present. Future. We see all — and we choose our path."

Once the most enlightened of the factions, the Diamonds' Spire now lies shattered — yet their gift of foresight endures. They see the flow of time, reading possible outcomes before they unfold. Strategic, elusive, and dangerously clever, they win battles long before they begin.

Playstyle:

  • Foresight & Prediction: Anticipate enemy moves and react before attacks land.
  • Manipulation: Change turn order, timing, or probability to alter outcomes.
  • Adaptive Combat: Gain strength from reading and countering patterns in enemy play.

Ideal For: Players who enjoy mind games, strategy, and staying three steps ahead.

♣

The Brutal Enforcers

"Mercy is weakness. Chaos brings strength."

Clubs are the merciless warlords of the Deck Realms — raw, unfiltered power made flesh. They fight dirty, strike hard, and thrive in the chaos they create. They live for destruction and domination, feeding on fear and bloodlust.

Playstyle:

  • Aggressive & Unrelenting: Heavy offense, crowd control, and overwhelming force.
  • Fear Mechanics: Intimidate opponents, lower enemy morale, and disrupt formations.
  • High Risk / High Reward: Big attacks that can backfire if mistimed or countered.

Ideal For: Players who love direct confrontation, chaos, and punishing overkill tactics.

Deck Realms: Factions at War – The Story Begins

Long before the war, the Realm of Decks thrived in harmony.
Four great Spires rose across the lands — monuments of life, energy, and balance.
Each pulsed with a different essence: Love, Honor, Might, and Foresight.

The Hearts, guided by the warmth of compassion, healed wounds both seen and unseen.

The Spades, noble and disciplined, upheld the realm's laws and defended the innocent.

The Clubs, forged in shadow and fire, thrived on survival, chaos, and strength.

The Diamonds, keepers of time and truth, watched over destiny itself.

Each faction guarded its Spire with four Councils — North, South, East, and West — thirteen warriors each, from the humble Two to the exalted Ace.
Together, they were the Deck, and for centuries, their order held the balance of the Realms.

The Shattering

No one knows who struck first.
Some say a Club brute marched on the borders of Heart's Vale.
Others whisper of a Diamond Seer who saw too far into a future she could not change.
But all agree on one thing:
When the Diamond Spire shattered, the balance of fate was torn apart.

Fragments of time scattered through the land, twisting reality.
What was once predictable became chaos.
Old alliances faltered. Trust decayed.
And from the cracks of madness, the Jokers emerged — four beings born of disorder, mocking every law and rule that once bound the Realms together.

They spread deceit like wildfire, pitting Faction against Faction, Spire against Spire.
Love turned to jealousy. Honor to arrogance. Strength to cruelty.
Even the Diamonds, seers of truth, could no longer see a single clear path.

The War of the Deck

Now, the Four Factions stand on the brink of annihilation.

The Hearts fight to heal what's left of the realm, their compassion hardening into fierce protection.
The Spades wage war for honor, believing only through discipline can order return.
The Clubs march to consume all — chaos is their creed, and they will forge a new world from the ashes.
The Diamonds seek fragments of their broken Spire, desperate to weave time together before it unravels completely.

And in the shadows, the Jokers laugh — twisting fate, shifting sides, ensuring that no Faction ever truly wins.

The Game Begins

In Deck Realms: Factions at War, you enter this world as a soldier of one of the Four Factions.
Your rank defines your strength.
Your allegiance defines your purpose.
Your choices — and the alliances you forge — will decide the future of the Realms.

The Spires are flickering.
The Councils are divided.
And somewhere, the Jokers are watching… waiting for their next move.

Because in Deck Realms — even the deck itself can turn against you.
